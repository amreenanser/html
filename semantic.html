Planets The Moon Fun Facts

🪐 The Planets

There are 8 planets in our solar system. Earth is the third one from the Sun! The largest planet is Jupiter and the smallest is Mercury.

🌕 The Moon

The Moon is Earth's only natural satellite. Did you know astronauts first walked on it in 1969?

🚀 Fun Facts

  • Sunlight takes 8 minutes to reach Earth.
  • Venus is the hottest planet!
  • Saturn could float in water because it's so light!

🌟 Did You Know?

Neptune has supersonic winds faster than 1,000 mph!

Pluto was reclassified as a dwarf planet in 2006.
